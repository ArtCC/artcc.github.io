Privacy Policy

Introduction

At MuseumAI Guide, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Information We Collect

  • Login data through Apple Sign-In, which may include anonymous login options.
  • Artworks or monuments scanned by you, linked only to your user ID for retrieval purposes.

No personal information such as your name or email is stored or associated with your scans.

How We Use Your Information

  • To allow you to retrieve and review scanned artworks or monuments.
  • To provide core functionality of the app.

We do not use analytics or tracking tools, and no data is used for profiling or advertisements.

Data Storage

Data about scanned artworks is stored in Firebase and is associated only with your anonymous user ID. This allows you to access your scan history. No personally identifiable information is linked to this data.

Sharing Information

We do not share your information with third parties. The app uses Firebase solely to facilitate login and data storage, and Firebase does not access or use your data for any other purpose.

Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your scan data at any time.
  • Revoke access or stop using the app without any obligations.

Security

We use Firebase's secure infrastructure to store your data. While we strive to protect your information, no system is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at contacto@arturocarreterocalvo.com.

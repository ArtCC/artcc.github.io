ScubaPlan Privacy Policy

By using the ScubaPlan application, you agree to the following privacy policy:

1. Introduction

At ScubaPlan, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This privacy policy explains how we handle your personal information.

2. Data Collection

ScubaPlan does not collect or store any personal user data on our servers. All data generated by the use of the application is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account. Apple login has the option to be anonymous, and Apple will link it to your real email. ScubaPlan uses Google's Firebase to facilitate this login implementation, and it is used solely and exclusively for managing the registered user's session.

3. Use of iCloud

ScubaPlan uses iCloud to store and synchronize your diving data across your devices. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is subject to Apple's privacy policy. We do not have access to this data stored in your iCloud.

4. Locally Stored Data

Your dive data, plans, and settings are stored locally on your device. This data is not shared with us or any third parties.

5. Access to Device Sensors

ScubaPlan accesses your device's sensors, such as the depth sensor on the Apple Watch (according to available and authorized models), exclusively to provide the application's functionalities. This data is not transmitted outside your device.

6. Analysis and Service Improvement

We do not use third-party analytics tools or collect usage data. Any improvements to our application are based on direct user feedback and App Store reviews.

7. Security

Although we do not store your data, we recommend that you keep your device and iCloud account secure to protect your personal data.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at: contacto@arturocarreterocalvo.com

By using ScubaPlan, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to be bound by this privacy policy.

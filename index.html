About Me

I'm a freelance Apple platform developer based in Madrid, Spain, specialized in creating innovative applications and tools for the entire Apple ecosystem (iOS, iPadOS, macOS, watchOS, tvOS).

Technical Experience

Over 10 years developing applications for Apple platforms (iOS, iPadOS, macOS, watchOS, tvOS) with Swift and Objective-C. Specialist in Firebase integration, REST APIs and MVVM and VIPER architectures.

Innovation

Creator of tools like Localizapp for Xcode and Votice SDK. I'm passionate about solving complex problems and creating solutions that positively impact the developer community.

Community

I actively contribute to the Apple development ecosystem through open source projects and technical content creation. Mentor for developers working with Apple platforms.

Achievements

Applications published on App Store with thousands of downloads. Creator of popular Xcode extensions and Stream Deck plugins used by developers worldwide.
